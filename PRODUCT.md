# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who want a premium weather app they can check quickly during the day, across phone, tablet, and desktop. They care about clear conditions, saved cities, comparison workflows, and a calm interface that feels precise rather than playful.

## Product Purpose
ATMOS helps users understand current weather and short-term forecast conditions with elegant presentation and low friction. Success means the interface feels trustworthy, fast to scan, and polished enough that weather data feels curated instead of dumped.

## Brand Personality
Editorial, precise, atmospheric. The tone should feel calm, premium, and technically sharp, with dark surfaces, restrained copy, and a sense of nocturnal focus.

## Anti-references
Do not make it look like a generic SaaS dashboard, a bright travel app, or a playful consumer card grid. Avoid loud gradients, bulky marketing sections, neon-on-black sci-fi treatment, and cheerful pastel weather visuals.

## Design Principles
1. Lead with atmosphere, but keep the task readable.
2. Make weather data feel curated through hierarchy, not decoration.
3. Use motion to support transitions and focus, never to distract.
4. Keep product controls familiar, dense enough for repeated use, and consistent across surfaces.
5. Treat dark mode as the native environment, not as an alternate skin.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard accessibility across all primary flows. Respect reduced-motion preferences, avoid color-only meaning, and keep form controls large enough for touch on mobile devices.
